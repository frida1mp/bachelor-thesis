Tendering & Quotation

  • Tender documents must be clear, complete, and consistent.

  • Define submission deadlines and evaluation timelines upfront.

  • Late or incomplete submissions should be handled according to defined rules.

  • Clarifications must be shared equally with all relevant suppliers.

  • All received quotations must be stored in the system of record.

Closure & Continuous Improvement

  • Confirm completion of contractual obligations before closure.

  • Ensure all invoices are accurate and approved prior to payment.

  • Close procurement records in the system once completed.

  • Capture lessons learned and improvement actions.

  • Use feedback to enhance future procurement processes.
